# Shipping Policy

## Delivery Time
- Standard delivery: 3-5 business days
- Express delivery: 1-2 business days

## Shipping Charges
- Free shipping on orders above ₹999
- ₹49 charge for orders below ₹999

## Order Tracking
Customers receive a tracking link via SMS and email after dispatch.

## Delays
Delivery may be delayed due to:
- Weather conditions
- High demand periods
- Logistics issues

## International Shipping
Currently, international shipping is not available.
