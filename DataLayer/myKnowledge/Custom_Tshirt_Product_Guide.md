# Custom T-Shirt Product Guide

Our ecommerce platform offers a wide range of customizable t-shirts, allowing customers to express their unique style and personality. Whether you're looking for a single personalized tee or bulk orders for events, teams, or businesses, we provide high-quality, comfortable, and durable options.

## T-Shirt Materials

We use premium fabrics to ensure comfort and longevity:

- **100% Cotton:** Soft, breathable, and ideal for everyday wear. Available in various weights (e.g., 180 GSM, 200 GSM).
- **Cotton-Polyester Blends:** Combines the softness of cotton with the durability and wrinkle resistance of polyester. Common blends include 60% cotton/40% polyester and 50% cotton/50% polyester.
- **Organic Cotton:** Grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, making it an eco-friendly choice.
- **Tri-Blend:** A mix of cotton, polyester, and rayon for a super-soft, lightweight feel.

## T-Shirt Styles

Choose from a variety of styles to suit your needs:

- **Classic Crew Neck:** Timeless and versatile, suitable for both men and women.
- **V-Neck:** A stylish alternative to the crew neck, offering a more formal look.
- **Scoop Neck:** A wider, more feminine neckline, popular among women.
- **Oversized/Fit:** Relaxed or fitted options to match your preferred silhouette.
- **Long Sleeve:** For cooler weather or layered looks.
- **Sleeveless/Tank Tops:** Perfect for warm climates or athletic wear.
