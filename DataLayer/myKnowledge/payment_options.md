# Payment Options

## Available Methods
- Credit/Debit Cards
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
- Net Banking
- Cash on Delivery (COD)

## COD Rules
- COD available only for orders below ₹5000
- ₹30 additional charge applies

## Payment Failures
If payment fails:
- Amount is auto-refunded within 3-5 business days
- Users can retry the payment

## Security
All transactions are secured using encrypted payment gateways.
