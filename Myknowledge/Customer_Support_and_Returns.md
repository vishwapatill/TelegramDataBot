# Customer Support and Returns

At our custom t-shirt ecommerce platform, your satisfaction is our top priority. We strive to provide excellent customer support and a hassle-free return process. Below, you’ll find information on how to contact us, our return and exchange policies, and answers to frequently asked questions.

## Contacting Customer Support

Our support team is available to assist you with any questions or concerns. You can reach us through the following channels:

- **Live Chat:** Available on our website during business hours (9 AM to 9 PM IST, Monday through Saturday).
- **Email:** Support@YourTshirtStore.com. We aim to respond within 24 hours.
- **Phone:** +91 12345 67890 (available 10 AM to 6 PM IST, Monday through Friday).
- **Social Media:** Message us on Instagram, Facebook, or Twitter for quick assistance.

## Order Tracking

Once your order is confirmed, you’ll receive an order confirmation email with a tracking number. Use this number to track your shipment on our website or the courier’s tracking page.

## Return and Exchange Policy

We want you to love your custom t-shirt. If you’re not completely satisfied, we offer returns and exchanges under the following conditions:

- **Eligibility:** Items must be returned within 14 days of delivery, in their original condition, unworn, and with tags attached.
- **Custom/Personalized Items:** Due to the custom nature of our products, we generally do not accept returns or exchanges unless there is a defect or error on our part.
- **Defective/Damaged Items:** If your t-shirt arrives defective or damaged, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the issue. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.
- **Wrong Size/Color:** If you received the wrong size or color due to our error, we will replace it at no additional cost.

## How to Initiate a Return or Exchange

1. **Contact Support:** Reach out to our customer support team with your order number and reason for return/exchange.
2. **Provide Details:** Share photos of the item and any relevant information.
3. **Approval:** Once approved, you’ll receive a return shipping label or instructions.
4. **Ship Back:** Send the item back to us using the provided label.
5. **Processing:** Upon receipt, we’ll inspect the item and process your refund or exchange within 5-7 business days.

## Refund Process

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Processing times vary by payment provider:

- **Credit/Debit Cards:** 3-5 business days.
- **Net Banking/UPI:** 1-3 business days.
- **Wallets:** Instant to 24 hours.

## Common Issues and Solutions

- **Print Quality Issues:** If the print is faded, misaligned, or peeling, contact us immediately for a replacement.
- **Sizing Problems:** Refer to our sizing guide before ordering. If you’re unsure, order a size up or contact us for advice.
- **Shipping Delays:** While we strive for timely delivery, delays can occur due to weather, customs, or courier issues. Track your order and contact us if it’s significantly delayed.

## FAQs

**Q: Can I cancel or modify my order?**
A: Orders can be canceled or modified within 24 hours of placement. After that, we cannot guarantee changes, as production may have already begun.

**Q: How long does production take?**
A: Standard production time is 3-5 business days, plus shipping time.

**Q: Do you offer international shipping?**
A: Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

**Q: Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?**
A: Yes, we offer sample orders for bulk customers. Contact our sales team for details.

**Q: What if my design looks different on the t-shirt than on the preview?**
A: While we strive for accuracy, colors may vary slightly due to screen settings and fabric textures. If the difference is significant, contact us for a resolution.
