# T-Shirt Customization Process

Our user-friendly customization tool makes it easy to design your perfect t-shirt. Follow this step-by-step guide to create a unique, personalized garment.

## Step 1: Select Your T-Shirt

Begin by choosing your preferred t-shirt style, material, color, and size. Use the product guide and sizing chart to make an informed selection.

## Step 2: Choose Your Design Method

You have two main options for customization:

1. **Start from Scratch:** Use our blank canvas to add text, graphics, and other elements.
2. **Use a Template:** Browse our library of pre-made designs and templates for inspiration.

## Step 3: Add Text

- **Enter Your Text:** Type your desired text (e.g., name, quote, slogan).
- **Font Selection:** Choose from a variety of fonts, including classic, modern, and decorative styles.
- **Text Color:** Select a color that contrasts well with your t-shirt.
- **Text Effects:** Apply effects like shadow, outline, or curvature for added flair.

## Step 4: Add Graphics

- **Upload Your Own Image:** Supported formats include JPEG, PNG, and SVG. Ensure your image is high-resolution for best results.
- **Browse Our Library:** Access thousands of clipart, icons, and illustrations.
- **Adjust Size and Position:** Drag and resize your graphics to fit your design.

## Step 5: Customize Colors

- **T-Shirt Color:** Change the base color of your t-shirt at any time.
- **Print Colors:** Adjust the colors of your text and graphics to match your vision.

## Step 6: Preview Your Design

Use the 3D preview tool to see how your design will look on the actual t-shirt. Rotate the view to check the front, back, and sides.

## Step 7: Save and Order

- **Save Your Design:** Create an account to save your design for future edits or reorders.
- **Add to Cart:** Select your quantity and proceed to checkout.
- **Review Order:** Double-check your design, size, and shipping details before finalizing your purchase.

## Tips for Best Results

- **Image Quality:** Use high-resolution images (300 DPI or higher) for crisp, clear prints.
- **Color Contrast:** Ensure your text and graphics stand out against the t-shirt color.
- **Design Placement:** Avoid placing designs too close to seams or edges.
- **Font Legibility:** Choose fonts that are easy to read, especially for text-heavy designs.

## Bulk Orders and Group Customization

For events, teams, or businesses, our platform supports bulk orders with group customization options. You can:
- Create a shared design for all participants.
- Allow individuals to personalize their own t-shirts within set parameters.
- Take advantage of volume discounts for large orders.
